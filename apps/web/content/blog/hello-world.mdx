# Hello from Cobalt

Welcome to the Cobalt blog. We'll use this space for product updates, notes on
what we're building, and the occasional essay on personal finance.

## Why a blog

Cobalt is built on a transparency-first posture — the source is open, the
roadmap is visible, and we want a plain-English channel to explain what's
changing and why.

## What to expect

- Product updates when we ship something new
- Design notes when we change something visible
- Occasional deeper posts on how we think about money

Thanks for reading.
